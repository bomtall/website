Intro

I am currently and have been a Painter & Decorator for 11 years. I work with my Father, our family have been proffesional decorators for over 100 years in Harrogate, breaking only when my grandfather joined the RAF during WWII.

Recently I have begun studying Mathematics and Computer Science. I am hoping for a degree apprenticeship so I can work and learn at the same time in the field of computer science.

Programming

Check out my A-Level Computer Science Project 'RemoteQuote':. A program which can estimate the price of painting & decorating work to be undertaken by a professional. You can use the optimisation feature which utilises a 0/1 Knapsack algorithm which I implemented. The project is coded using Python.
